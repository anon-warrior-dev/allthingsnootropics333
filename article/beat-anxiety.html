Quick intro ‚Äî what is social anxiety and how nootropics can help

Social anxiety means feeling nervous or self-conscious in social situations ‚Äî meeting new people, speaking in public, or attending events. When it limits daily life it becomes a problem: avoiding chances, missing work, or eroding confidence.

There are two complementary routes: skills-based (therapy, exposure, breathwork) and biological supports (sleep, diet, targeted supplements). Nootropics are not a cure, but they can reduce the physical noise ‚Äî heart racing, shaky hands, racing thoughts ‚Äî so you can actually use practice and exposure to build long-term confidence.

Rule of thumb: start low, try one supplement at a time for 7‚Äì14 days, and keep a short log (sleep, mood, social events, any side effects).
